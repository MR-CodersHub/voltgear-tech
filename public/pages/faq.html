FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about our products and services. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our support team.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping options are available at checkout for 1-2 day delivery.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all our products. Items must be in their original packaging and condition.

Do products come with a warranty?

Yes, all TechGear hardware comes with a standard 2-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to follow your package in real-time.
